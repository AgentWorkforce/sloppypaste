sloppypaste

[slah-pee-payst]

noun, verb

  1. Pasting raw AI output without reading it, editing it, or pulling out the useful part.
  2. Making someone else do the work of figuring out what you meant.

You may have just sloppypasted someone. This page exists as a polite heads-up because the mistake is common, understandable, and easy to fix.

Read it before you share it. Don't be a slop cannon.

Alternative approach

Avoid sloppy pastes altogether.

If the LLMs need to compare notes, let them communicate directly and hand you the summary. Fewer giant transcripts. More signal. Less paste-shaped chaos.
